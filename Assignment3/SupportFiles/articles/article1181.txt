kuwaiti oil exports said not affected by gulf war kuwait oct kuwaits oil exports have not been affected by the sevenyear iraniraq war kuwait oil tanker company kotc chairman and managing director abdul fattah albader told a kuwaiti newspaper kuwait has exploited all available opportunities to continue exporting its oil without any reduction the alanbaa newspaper quoted him as saying he said kotc made profits of more than two mln dinars seven mln dlrs in the fiscal year ending last june but predicted lower profits this year due to higher costs for chartering and operating vessels reuter
