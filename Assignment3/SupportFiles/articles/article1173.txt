thai traders plan maize futures market bangkok oct thai traders plan to establish a company to regulate maize forward trading in what could be a first step towards a commodities futures exchange maize dealers said traders and internal trade department officials agreed last week to commission a study on a structure to regulate maize forward trading and to set up a company thailand commodity exchange co ltd with businesses as shareholders who will act as brokers in the futures market chanthong pattamapong a commodities trader asked to to draw up the study said if the maize futures market succeeds it may be extended to other commodities perhaps sugar and rubber reuter
