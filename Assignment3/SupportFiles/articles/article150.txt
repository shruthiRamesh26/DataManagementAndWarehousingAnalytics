mexico closes border to some us fruit washington oct mexico has decided to close its border to imports of fresh fruit from san diego and orange counties in california the us agriculture departments officer in mexico city said in a field report the report dated october said the border closing became effective september and also includes the surrounding mile area of the two counties while the ban applies specifically to orange county fruits that are hosts of the mediterranean fruit fly and san diego county fruits that are hosts of the oriental fruit fly the mexican government will stop all fresh fruit shipments from the counties until proof of noninfestation is provided the report said reuter
