eep dairy cattle initiative to egypt withdrawn washington oct the us agriculture department has withdrawn the offers of dairy cattle to egypt under the export enhancement program the department said the initiative for head of dairy cattle was announced on september and sales of head have been approved since that date the department said it is its objective to complete sales within one year after announcing an initiative and that initiatives are continued for over a year only if sufficient sales activity occurs it said it is giving exporters three days notice of the withdrawn in keeping with regulations under the program reuter
