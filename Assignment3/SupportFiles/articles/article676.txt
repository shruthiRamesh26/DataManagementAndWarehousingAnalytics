venezuela backs increase in ecuador opec quota caracas oct venezuela will back ecuadors bid to raise its opec quota above barrels per day bpd because it is a justifiable demand venezuelan energy minister arturo hernandez grisanti said on monday he told reporters the country deserved a higher quota in compensation for the five months it was forced to suspend oil production when its main pipeline was destroyed by an earthquake last march for this fivemonth period ecuador did not exercise its right to produce bpd he said we should recognize ecuadors right to increase its quota there is a basis of justice in that countrys petition he added however the quota increase should be gradual to avoid harming the market he added hernandez grisanti said he understood ecuador would formally submit the request for a higher quota at the next ministerial conference of the organization of petroleum exporting countries opec opening december in vienna reuter
