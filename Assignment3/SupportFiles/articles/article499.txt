brooklyn union ltbu to buy gas from shell oil new york oct brooklyn union gas co said it has contracted to purchase up to mln cubic feet of natural gas per day from shell oil co a royal dutchshell group ltrd ltsc subsidiary brooklyn union said the longterm contract will cover about pct of its total supplies a spokesman said shell and the utility agreed to not disclose the length of the contract or the price of the gas which he described as competitive with a market sensitive escalation formula brooklyn union said the gas from shell oil will replace about pct of the gas previously supplied by transco energy incs lte transcontinental gas pipe line corp subsidiary the utility said transcontinental will transport the gas being purchased from shell oil possibly beginning as soon as november reuter
