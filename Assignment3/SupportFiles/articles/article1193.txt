bonn ministry has no comment on baker remarks bonn oct the west german finance ministry declined to comment on weekend criticism by us treasury secretary james baker of recent west german interest rate increases baker said the us would reexamine the february louvre accord to stabilise currencies reached by leading industrial democracies the rise in west germany short term interest rates was not in the spirit of an agreement by these nations in washington which reaffirmed the louvre pact he said a finance ministry spokesman asked for an official ministry reaction to bakers remarks said he could make no comment reuter
