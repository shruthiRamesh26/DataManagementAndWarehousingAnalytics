att ltt supports fcc plan on price ceilings washington oct american telephone and telegraph co said it supported the federal communications commissions fcc plan to establish price ceilings for att longdistance services as a replacement for current regulation the company was responding to an fcc proposal of august that price caps replace the current rateofreturn regulation which has been in place for the past years att said vigorous competition in the longdistance industry has made obsolete rateofreturn which stipulates that a company can earn no more than a fixed rate currently pct on its investment the company termed the price cap a transitional step and said it hoped the fcc would support a program of regulatory oversight which would consist of a streamlined tariff process reuter
