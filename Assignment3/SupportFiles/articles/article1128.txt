kansas city exchanges audit trail adequate washington oct the commodity futures trading commission cftc said the kansas city board of trades kcbt oneminute audit trail system was adequate and accurate but in its second rule enforcement review of an exchanges audit trail system cftc said kcbt needed to provide a more accurate indication of the percent of trades that meet the oneminute timing standard cftc also recommended the exchange develop a computerized program to compare time and sales prints to the record of trades in its trade register reuter
