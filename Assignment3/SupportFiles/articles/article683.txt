cardiac control ltccsco in marketing pact palm coast fla oct cardiac control systems inc said it entered into a marketing agreement with a japanese firm to distribute cardiacs pacemakers in japan cardiac said the agreement requires the japanese firm to purchase certain minimum quantities of pacemakers annually over the next three years with the aggregate value of purchases of about mln dlrs cardiac said it received an initial order from the japanese company which is did not name for about mln dlrs and expects to begin shipments during its fourth quarter to end march reuter
