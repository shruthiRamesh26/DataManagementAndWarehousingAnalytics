2  GOLDMAN SACHS DENIES RUMOURS OF SOLVENCY PROBLEM       LONDON Oct 20    ltGoldman Sachs and Co is not facing severe financial problems and has not filed for protection under US Bankruptcy laws Robert Conway managing director of Goldman Sachs International Corp said     He was responding to a Reuter enquiry about rumours in London financial markets that the firm was in financial trouble     There is absolutely positively no truth to those rumours he said noting that in the current financial environment rumours like this are bound to surface     Conway said that Goldman Sachs has had an excellent year and that it has a strong balance sheet and good liquidity     You cant be in the equity market as we are and not experience some problems after a day like yesterday when the Dow Jones Industrial Average dropped 508 points But we are not facing any severe financial problems he added  REUTER 3
