2  IRAN SAYS US NAVAL FORCES ATTACKED TWO PLATFORMS       NICOSIA Oct 19    Iran said US Naval forces attacked two of its oil platforms in the southern Gulf on Monday afternoon the Iranian news agency IRNA reported     IRNA received in Nicosia said an informed source at the Iranian oil ministry identified the two platforms as Resalat and Reshadat about 60 miles from Irans Lavan island oil storage site     Regional shipping sources earlier said three Iranian offshore oil sites at Sassan Rostam and Rakhsh had been attacked at 700 AM 0300 GMT on Monday by unidentified aircraft     In Washington US Defence Secretary Caspar Weinberger said four US Destroyers attacked and destroyed an Iranian oil platform about 120 miles east of Bahrain  REUTER 3
