2  IRAN SOVIETS TO HOLD TALKS ON OIL EXCHANGE DEAL       MOSCOW Oct 20    Iran and the Soviet Union have provisionally agreed to hold talks on a possible exchange of Iranian crude oil for finished Soviet oil products an official spokesman said Tuesday     Gennady Gerasimov chief of the Soviet Foreign Ministrys Information Directorate told reporters the agreement had been reached during a visit to Moscow last week by Irans Oil Minister Gholamreza Aqazadeh     Gerasimov said no date had been set for the talks which would also study the possibility of Soviet technical assistance for Irans oil industry  REUTER 3
