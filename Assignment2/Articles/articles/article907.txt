2  US SENATE PANEL VOTES TO LIMIT COUNTY LOAN DROP       WASHINGTON Oct 19    The Senate Agriculture committee voted to limit changes in county loan rate differentials starting with the 1988 crop as part of a budget deficit reduction package     The panel also approved measures that could trigger larger corn and wheat acreage reduction requirements increase farmerheld reserve storage payments reduce a potential milk support price cut and require advance deficiency payments for producers of major crops     A proposal to require imported tropical oils be labeled on US food products failed by a 108 vote  Reuter 3
