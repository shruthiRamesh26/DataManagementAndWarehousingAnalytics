2  JAPAN MONEY GROWTH TO STAY AT 1112 PCT  OFFICIAL       TOKYO Oct 20    Growth in Japans M2 plus certificates of deposit CD money supply in the October to December period is not expected to accelerate but will remain at high levels between 11 and 12 pct a senior Bank of Japan official said     The central bank will keep a watch on high growth in liquidity because this is a factor that may cause rises in prices of goods he said     The September growth of 111 pct year on year announced earlier today should not be taken as implying that the money supply has started to expand very rapidly he said In August the rate of increase was 110 pct  REUTER 3
