2  FINNISH STATE LAUNCHES BONDS FOR BILLION MARKKA       HELSINKI Oct 19    The Finnish State launched two bonds totalling one billion markka with loan periods of 10 years and five years and annual interest rates of 775 and 725 pct respectively     The bonds sell concurrently until the total is reached and nominal values are 25000 markka 10000 markka 5000 markka and 1000 markka the Finance Ministry said     It was decided with the banks the rates should be 025 pct less than the eight and 75 pct rates respectively of 10year and fiveyear bonds launched by the State on August 17 a Ministry statement said  REUTER 3
