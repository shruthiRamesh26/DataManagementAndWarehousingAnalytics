2  MEXICO PUTS FOREIGN INVESTMENT AT 178 BILLION DLRS       MEXICO CITY Oct 20    Foreign investment in Mexico stood at end August at 178 billion dlrs following an increase of 900 mln dlrs in the first eight months the Trade and Industrial Development Ministry said     It said authorized investments rose 30 pct last year equivalent to 245 billion dlrs and expected a similar increase for 1987 as a whole  Reuter 3
