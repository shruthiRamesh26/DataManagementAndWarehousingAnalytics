2  WEST GERMANY STILL COMMITTED TO LOUVRE ACCORD       BONN Oct 19    West German government sources said Bonn remained committed to the Louvre Accord to stabilise currencies which was struck by leading western democracies in Paris last February     Over the weekend US Treasury Secretary James Baker criticised recent rises in West German shortterm interest rates and said such developments were not in the spirit of the Louvre pact He said the agreement may have to be reexamined     The sources said the West German interest rate rises had to be seen in the context of interest rate developments worldwide  Reuter 3
