2  TANZANIAN COTTON THREATENED BY LACK OF STORAGE       DAR ES SALAAM Oct 20    About 60000 tonnes of harvested raw cotton may be spoiled by rain in Tanzanias northern Shinyanga region because it is stored in the open or in crude village sheds radio Tanzania reported     The cotton worth one billion shillings cannot be moved to ginneries in the region because most mill warehouses are full Many mills are not working because of a lack of spare parts it added     Agriculture Ministry officials have forecast a 198788 cotton harvest of about 200000 tonnes down from 215000 in 198687  REUTER 3
