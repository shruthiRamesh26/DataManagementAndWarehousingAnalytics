2  PROTESTERS MARCH AGAINST PRICE RISES IN SUDAN       KHARTOUM Oct 19    About 10000 people marched through the Sudanese capital Monday in protest against price increases and devaluation of the Sudanese pound     The march was aimed at measures agreed with the International Monetary Fund including devaluation of the Sudanese pound by 44 pct and a rise in sugar and petrol prices     The devaluation was announced on October 3 Sudans foreign debt costs an estimated one billion dlrs a year to service  REUTER 3
