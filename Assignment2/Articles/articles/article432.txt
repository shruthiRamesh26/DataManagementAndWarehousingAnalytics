2  PACIFIC STOCK EXHCANGE TO CLOSE HALF HOUR EARLY       SAN FRANCISCO Oct 20    The Pacific Stock Exchange said it will close one half hour early at 1300 pacific daylight time due to heavy volume and to coincide with the closing of other US exchanges     The exchange closed a half hour early yesterday as well as both traders and computers had difficulty keeping up with orders     A stock exchange spokeswoman said at 1145 pdt the latest estimate on volume at the exchange was 9147000 shares     Yesterday the exchange traded a record volume of over 17 mln shares  Reuter 3
