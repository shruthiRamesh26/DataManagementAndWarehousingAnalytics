2  STRIKE HITS BRAZILS LARGEST AUTO MANUFACTURER       SAO PAULO Oct 20    An indefinite strike by 47000 carworkers on Tuesday closed most Brazilian plants of Autolatina the company said     Autolatina one of the worlds largest automakers said it had offered its employees a pay rise of 2066 pct adding that the workers were pressing for a 659 pct rise     The strike is the first to hit operations of Autolatina since it formally came into existence on July 1 The company groups Ford and Volkswagen in Brazil and Argentina     A spokesman said factories in Argentina were unaffected He said about 9000 of the companys 55900 employees in Brazil were still working and some production was continuing  Reuter 3
