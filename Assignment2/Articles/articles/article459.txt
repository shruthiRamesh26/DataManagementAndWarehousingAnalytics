2  WEST VIRGINIA FREE OF TWO MAJOR CATTLE DISEASES       WASHINGTON Oct 20    West Virginia has been declared free of tuberculosis and brucellosis from its cattle population the US Agriculture Department said     It said a state is recognized as tuberculosisfree if no case of the disease is diagnosed for at least five years and if the state complies with the uniform methods and rules of the statefederal cooperative eradication program     To achieve brucellosisfree status a states cattle population must remain uninfected for the previous 12 months     There are 33 states plus the US Virgin Islands classified as free of bovine tuberculosis and 24 states plus Puerto Rico and the US Virgin Islands which have eradicated brucellosis Only 20 states and the US Virgin islands have eradicated both diseases it noted  Reuter 3
