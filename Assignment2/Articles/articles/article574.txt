2  CANADA PANEL ADVISES CUTTING US CORN DUTY       OTTAWA Oct 20    The Canadian Import Tribunal said the countervailing duty on US corn imports should be cut to 30 Canadian cts a bushel from 110 dlrs a bushel     In a report to the Canadian finance department the tribunal said the duty is hurting Canadian farmers and food processors The duty was imposed last year after the revenue department found US corn production was subsidized     It is now up to the government to decide whether to change the duty  Reuter 3
