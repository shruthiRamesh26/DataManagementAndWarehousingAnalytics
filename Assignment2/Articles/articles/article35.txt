2  RICE RESEARCH INSTITUTE NAMES NEW HEAD       MANILA Oct 20    The Manilabased International Rice Research Institute IRRI said West German agricultural scientist Klaus Lampe will take over as its directorgeneral in early 1988 succeeding MS Swaminathan     An IRRI statement said Lampe 56 is currently senior adviser to the German Agency for Technical Cooperation at Eschborn and was a former head of the agriculture section of the Federal Ministry for Economic Cooperation     It said Swaminathan who has headed IRRI since 1982 will concentrate on environmental and agricultural issues  REUTER 3
