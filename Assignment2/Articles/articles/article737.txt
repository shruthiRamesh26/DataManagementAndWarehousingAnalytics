2  US CHAMBER SAYS STOCK DROP HAS RUN COURSE       WASHINGTON Oct 19    The US Chamber of Commerce in a statement said the phenomenal selloff in the stock market is very likely to have run its course     The chamber said there is no justification for such a dramatic downward correction     While conceding that there is some justifiable concern about inflation higher interest rates and a lower dollar these uncertainties have been overcompensated for over recent weeks the Chamber said  Reuter 3
