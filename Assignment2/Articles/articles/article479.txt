2  USDA REPORTS CORN SOLD TO USSR       WASHINGTON Oct 20    Private exporters reported sales of 125000 tonnes of US corn to the Soviet Union for delivery during the 198788 season and under the fifth year of the Long Term Grain Supply Agreement     The department noted the sales are the first reported for delivery during the fifth year of the Agreement which began October 1 1987     Sales of wheat and corn to the USSR for delivery during the fourth year of the agreement totaled 8182500 tonnes  4080500 tonnes of wheat and 4102300 tonnes of corn In addition sales of soybeans totaled 68200 tonnes it said  Reuter 3
