2  DEUTSCHE BANK CHIEF SAYS LOUVRE PACT STILL INTACT       FRANKFURT Oct 19    Deutsche Bank AG joint chief executive Friedrich Wilhelm Christians said he believed the Louvre accord on currency stability was still intact     Christians told a news conference he met US Treasury Secretary James Baker in the last two weeks after short term German interest rates had risen twice     I am sure that with 17720 marks the dollar is still within the Louvre agreement I do not see that the accord has been terminated Christians said He was responding to questions about comments by Baker who said the Louvre accord was still operative but criticised rises in West German interest rates  REUTER 3
